Introduction to Intercultural Communication and Hypertext

As we continue to globalize the field of technical communication, we must also continue to study the way we learn about these new cultures and languages that we come across through our audiences, and how digital literacy and rhetoric play a role in the way we communicate across hypertext.

Understanding and appreciating other cultures is just one step necessary for succeeding in effective international or intercultural communication.

This site will also discuss the role of hypertext in teaching and learning about new languages and cultures, as learning about a people and their language is often one of many steps necessary for international communication.

Learning About Cultures, Translation, and Teaching a New Language — Teaching students about other languages and cultures, as well as how to write for translation, are all important factors for international technical communication.

Digital Literacy and Rhetoric — Digital literacy and rhetoric vary between borders and languages. It is important to understand how to navigate these differences when communicating interculturally.
